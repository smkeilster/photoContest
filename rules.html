2018 Arts in the Heart Photo Contest

The Clubhou.se Photo Contest entry rules:

  1. Eligibility: This Campaign is open only to those who sign up at the theClubhou.se Photo Contest booth at the Arts in the Heart of Augusta Festival and who are 18 years of age or older as of the date of entry. The Campaign is only open to legal residents of 50 U.S. states and the District of Columbia, and is void where prohibited by law. Employees of theCluhou.se, its affiliates, subsidiaries, advertising and promotion agencies, and suppliers, (collectively the “Employees”), and immediate family members and/or those living in the same household of Employees are not eligible to participate in the Campaign. The Campaign is subject to all applicable federal, state, and local laws and regulations. Void where prohibited.
  2. Agreement to Rules: By participating, the Contestant (“You”) agree to be fully unconditionally bound by these Rules, and You represent and warrant that You meet the eligibility requirements. In addition, You agree to accept the decisions of theClubhou.se as final and binding as it relates to the content of this Campaign.
  3. Campaign Period: Entries will be accepted online starting on Sept. 15, 2018, 5:00 ET and ending Sept. 16, 2018, 7:00 ET. All online entries must be received by Sept. 16, 2018, 7:00 ET.
  4. How to Enter: The Campaign must be entered by submitting an entry using the equipment provided at theCluhou.se booth at the Arts in the Heart of Augusta Festival. The entry must fulfill all Campaign requirements, as specified, to be eligible to win a prize. Entries that are incomplete or do not adhere to the rules or specifications may be disqualified at the sole discretion of theClubhou.se. If You use fraudulent methods or otherwise attempt to circumvent the rules, your submission may be removed from eligibility at the sole discretion of theClubhou.se.
